MEMORANDUM

TO: Departmental Welfare Directors
FROM: Office of the Faculty Welfare Director
DATE: January 20, 2026
SUBJECT: UPDATED REGISTRATION PROCEDURES FOR FRESHMEN

1. SUBMISSION OF ACCEPTANCE RECEIPTS

All freshmen are hereby notified that Acceptance Receipts should be submitted at their Department’s School Office, rather than the Bursary or Administrative offices. Please note that the receipt must be submitted alongside the Eligibility Form.

2. COLLECTION FOR PREVIOUS SUBMISSIONS

Students who previously submitted their receipts to other offices are required to gather for collection and redirection at the School Pavilion as per the schedule below:

• First Slot: 10:30 AM

• Second Slot: 02:30 PM (For those unable to make the morning session)

Welfare Directors are directed to ensure this information reaches every student within their respective jurisdictions immediately to avoid further delays in registration.
